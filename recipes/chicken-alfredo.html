Chicken Alfredo

A rich and creamy pasta dish made with tender chicken, fettuccine, and a luscious Alfredo sauce.

Prep: 10 mins
Cook: 20 mins
Total: 30 mins
Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless chicken breasts, sliced
  • 300g fettuccine pasta
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook fettuccine according to package directions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, melt butter over medium heat and cook chicken until browned and cooked through. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Add garlic and sauté for 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Pour in heavy cream and bring to a simmer for 3–5 minutes.
  5. Stir in parmesan cheese and mix until smooth.
  6. Add the pasta to the skillet and toss to coat evenly.
  7. Garnish with chopped parsley and serve hot.
