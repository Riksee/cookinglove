Creamy Tomato Pasta

⏱️ Prep: 10 min
🔥 Cook: 15 min
🍽️ Servings: 2
⭐ Difficulty: Easy
#Pasta #Creamy #Dinner

📝 Ingredients

200g pasta
1 cup tomato puree
½ cup heavy cream
2 cloves garlic (minced)
Salt & pepper to taste
Fresh basil leaves
Grated parmesan

👨‍🍳 Instructions

Step 1: Boil pasta until al dente. Drain and set aside.

Step 2: Sauté garlic in olive oil until fragrant.

Step 3: Add tomato puree, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 5 minutes.

Step 4: Stir in heavy cream and simmer for 3 more minutes.

Step 5: Toss pasta with sauce. Cook 2 minutes more.

Step 6: Garnish with basil and parmesan. Serve warm.

🍎 Nutrition Facts (per serving)

  • Calories: 420 kcal
  • Carbs: 45g
  • Protein: 12g
  • Fat: 22g
  • Fiber: 3g
  • Sugar: 5g
